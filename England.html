Welcome to England

This green land, this sceptred isle, this crucible of empire and pioneer of parliamentary democracy, is the most eccentric, extraordinary and downright intriguing place on Earth.An excursion through England is a voyage through history. History that you can feel and re-live. You can lay your hands on the antiquated stone monuments of a 5000-year-old stone circle, or walk the bulwarks of a medieval fortress – similarly as they were watched by networking mail-clad protectors numerous hundreds of years back. Visit the destinations of the legend of Lord Arthur, the works of Shakespeare and the royal residences of rulers – at various times. While England has developed a culture and tradition that may appear complex, much of it will be familiar – on the surface at least – to many visitors, thanks to the vast catalogue of British pop music, films and TV programs that have been exported around the world
